What is this game

A story about becoming

Echoes of Valor is a choice-driven visual novel set in the Philippines. You play as a young man who enlists in the military — not for glory, but for family. For duty. For something he cannot yet name.

Every decision shapes who you become and whether you come back whole. The game explores the emotional cost of service: the relationships left behind, the fear that never disappears, and the brotherhood forged in hardship.

The story

Five chapters.
One soldier's truth.

A complete arc of transformation, told through the choices you make at every turn.

01

Letting Go

The Weight of Enlistment

A morning that feels heavier than usual. You say goodbye to your mother and best friend, then board a bus toward something you cannot take back. At camp, Sgt. Ramirez makes one thing clear: the person you were is no longer welcome here.

02

Trust & Survival

Fire and Brotherhood

Live-fire exercises turn chaos into consequence. Here you meet Luis — your closest bond in uniform. When he goes down mid-exercise, you face a choice that defines what kind of soldier you are.

03

Fear & Control

Into the Unknown

The squad deploys on a real mission. Silence replaces shouting. Every shadow is a question. You learn that courage is not the absence of fear — it is moving forward despite it.

04

Sacrifice

The Breaking Point

Inside a fortified position. Explosions, confusion, fragmented orders. A grenade. A split second. No perfect choice exists — only the one you make and the weight of it afterward.

05

Identity & Meaning

Echoes

After the mission, everything slows. You reflect on home, on who you were, on what you've lost and kept. Three endings wait — each honest, none easy, all shaped by the choices you've made.

How it's played

Choices, not controllers

Read, decide, and live with the result.

01

Read the Dialogue

Scenes unfold line by line. Click or press Enter to advance through each moment before you act.

02

Make Your Choice

Two to three options appear. Correct paths are never colour-coded — you must read, not guess.

03

Earn Stars

Performance is rated by efficiency. Three stars means you read the room correctly every time.

04

Unlock Chapters

Complete a chapter's levels to open the next. Decisions made early ripple through to the end.

05

Face the Endings

Three possible endings, shaped by the cumulative weight of your choices. No guaranteed happy ending.
